# Pricing Page & Tier Messaging (Revised)

## Current Pricing Concept (from design docs)
The existing billing spec shows: Free ($0), Starter ($19), Growth ($49), Enterprise (Custom)

**Problem:** These prices are too low for the value proposition. Nightlamp catches failures that would cost founders thousands in lost revenue, support hours, and churn. At $19-$49/mo, we compete with UptimeRobot — and that's the wrong comparison.

**Revised Pricing:** $99 / $299 / $499 per month

## The Framework: "Sleep Score"

Reposition from monitoring to peace of mind. Each tier is a "sleep level" — how well the founder can sleep knowing their app is covered.

- **Free tier is gone** — $99 minimum validates serious buyers
- **14-day free trial** on all plans (no credit card for Indie, card required for Growth+ to qualify leads)
- **"Most Popular"** badge on Growth ($299) — our expected highest volume tier
- **Annual discount:** 20% off on annual billing (displayed as "Save $X/yr")
- **App health audit** as trial kickoff — every new trial gets a manual audit within 48h (high-touch conversion)
