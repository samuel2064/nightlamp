# Pilot Agency Outreach Campaign

## Objective
Acquire 10 design agencies (5‑50 designers each) for the Magpie pilot, gather usage data, and generate early case‑studies.

## Target Segments
- **North America** – agencies using Figma or Sketch.
- **Europe** – agencies with strong design ops processes.
- **LATAM & APAC** – emerging agencies interested in AI‑assisted workflow.

## Channels & Assets
1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**
   - Personalized connection request (100‑150 words) referencing recent work.
   - Follow‑up InMail (3‑4 paragraphs) highlighting:
     * AI‑powered screenshot tagging.
     * Visual‑similarity search saves up to 30 % time.
     * Free 30‑day pilot with priority support.
   - Include one‑page PDF overview (see `pilot-agency-overview.pdf`).
2. **Email Outreach**
   - Subject: *“Boost your design workflow with AI‑powered screenshot organization”*
   - Body: Brief intro, value proposition, pilot invite link, call‑to‑action to schedule a demo.
   - CTA button linking to `/pilot-signup`.
3. **Community Forums** (Designer Slack groups, Reddit r/DesignOps)
   - Short post introducing Magpie, pilot benefits, and link to sign‑up form.
   - Pin a comment with FAQ.

## Messaging Pillars
- **Efficiency** – “Find the exact screenshot in seconds, not minutes.”
- **AI Insight** – “Automatic tagging surfaces hidden patterns across projects.”
- **Zero‑Risk** – “Free pilot, no credit‑card required, dedicated onboarding.”

## Timeline (4 weeks)
| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1 | Finalise PDF overview, prepare LinkedIn list (200 prospects). |
| 2 | Launch LinkedIn connection requests + first InMail batch. |
| 3 | Send email blast to curated agency list (via HubSpot). |
| 4 | Post in community forums, follow‑up with interested leads, schedule demos. |

## Success Metrics
- **Leads**: 30 qualified agency contacts (≥ 10 % conversion to pilot sign‑up).
- **Sign‑ups**: 10 agencies complete the pilot onboarding.
- **Engagement**: ≥ 5 demo meetings per week.
- **Feedback**: Collect NPS & usage stats after 2 weeks.
