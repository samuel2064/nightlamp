## Positioning by Segment

### For Accelerators
**Problem:** Portfolio companies ship fast but break silently. Partners spend hours debugging trivial issues (expired keys, rate limits) instead of mentoring.

**Nightlamp white-label solution:**
- Onboard portfolio companies in < 1 hour per app
- Partner dashboards: see all portfolio health at a glance
- Automatic fixes for common failures
- Quarterly portfolio health report (great for LP reporting)

**Pitch angle:** "One partner told us they spent 40% of their time on firefighting. Nightlamp cuts that to zero."

**Package:** $999/mo for up to 25 portfolio companies, $50/mo per additional

### For No-Code Agencies
**Problem:** You built the app. Client assumes it's "done." But API keys expire, webhooks break, rate limits shift. Clients blame you, you eat the unbilled support hours.

**Nightlamp white-label solution:**
- Add "managed maintenance" as a line item in every proposal
- Client sees *your* brand on monitoring, not another SaaS tool
- Auto-fix for 80% of common failures (you never touch code)
- You decide what to automate vs. what to bill manually

**Pitch angle:** "Add $200-500/mo to every client retainer and never do free debugging again."

**Package:** $999/mo for up to 20 client apps, $50/mo per additional

### For Dev Shops & Consultancies
**Problem:** You deliver. Then the app rots. Post-launch support is a margin killer.

**Nightlamp white-label solution:**
- Wrap monitoring into every delivery
- Post-launch health reports (looks professional, drives upsell)
- Proactive alerts reduce emergency calls
- Your brand, your client relationship

**Pitch angle:** "Include Nightlamp in your SOW. Your clients get 24/7 coverage. You get recurring revenue without hiring."
